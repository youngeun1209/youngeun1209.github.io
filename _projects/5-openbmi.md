I led the technical development of **OpenBMI**, a widely used open-source platform for reproducible BCI research.

- Standardized, reproducible, real-time pipelines for the MI, ERP, and SSVEP paradigms.
- Designed the evaluation methodology and metrics that compare decoding models on equal footing.
- Released the accompanying EEG dataset, published as a peer-reviewed data descriptor in GigaScience (~700 citations; 221 stars, 113 forks).
