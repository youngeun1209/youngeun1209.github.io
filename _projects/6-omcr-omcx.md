I build open-source multi-agent frameworks that bring agentic LLM workflows to scientific research.

- [OMCR](https://github.com/youngeun1209/oh-my-claudecode-research) (for Anthropic's Claude Code) and [OMCX](https://github.com/youngeun1209/oh-my-codex-research) (for OpenAI's Codex) orchestrate specialized LLM agents across the research lifecycle.
- Agents handle literature synthesis, hypothesis refinement, figure planning, manuscript revision, citation and evidence checking, and reviewer response.
- Designed with durable project state and evaluation-driven iteration.
